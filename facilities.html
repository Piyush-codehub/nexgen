Welcome to Our World-Class Facilities

At Nexgen Public School, we provide a wide range of state-of-the-art facilities to support the all-around development of our students. Explore our campus offerings designed for the best learning experience.

Learning Facilities

Modern classrooms, science labs and a well-stocked library for an enriching academic experience.

Sports Complex

Extensive sports grounds including football, basketball, cricket, and a swimming pool for physical fitness.

Computer Lab

A state-of-the-art computer lab with the latest technology and high-speed internet access.

Arts & Music Room

A creative space for music, dance, and art programs encouraging artistic expression.

Library

A quiet space with an extensive collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources.

Cafeteria

A hygienic cafeteria serving nutritious and balanced meals to our students.

Playground

Safe, well-maintained playgrounds with recreational structures for fun and physical activities.
